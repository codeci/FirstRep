Win Lose or Die - Dice Game

  1. Need a minimum of 1000 points on a roll before your points can accumulate
  2. Three of a kind are worth 100 points times the number rolled (Except 3 x 1s = 1000) *HAS TO BE SAME ROLL
  3. Four of a kind is worth double the three of a kind total *HAS TO BE SAME ROLL
  4. Five of a kind is worth double the four of a kind total *HAS TO BE SAME ROLL
  5. Straights are 1000 points (1,2,3,4,5 or 2,3,4,5,6) *HAS TO BE SAME ROLL
  6. First to 10,000 wins. (Must land on 10,000 exactly)
